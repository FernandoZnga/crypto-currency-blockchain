How It Works

Three simple steps.

1

Sign up

Create your account and get an educational wallet.

2

Complete KYC

Submit your identity details and wait for review.

3

Use the wallet

Receive tokens immediately and send them after approval.

This is an educational blockchain environment. No real funds are used, and every workflow exists for learning purposes only.

Documentation

What this demo includes.

Accounts and sessionsUsers can register, sign in, and access role-based screens for learning flows.
KYC workflowIdentity submissions move through pending, approved, denied, and resubmission states.
Wallets and transfersBalances are derived from chain activity, and verified users can send educational tokens.
Funding and network viewDemo purchases credit wallets on-chain, and the UI shows blockchain activity across nodes.
